Website Redesign for Image Civil & Electrical Engineering Ltd

We redesigned the website for Image Civil & Electrical Engineering Ltd, a construction company based in Ndola. Our goal was to create a modern, user-friendly website that effectively showcases the company's high-quality and efficient construction services, as well as their professional team of experts. The scope of the project included a comprehensive review of the current website, a new website design, improved layout and navigation, new content highlighting services and expertise and responsive design for mobile-friendliness. The result is a new website that will increase client satisfaction and lead generation, completed on time and within budget.
